What are Case Competitions?

Case Competitions are a great way for students to learn about an organization and potential opportunities, as well as network at the same time. Delta Sigma Pi is providing you with the unique opportunity to connect with recruiters and get your foot in the door, setting you up for your future.

Feel free to contact Delta Sigma Pi's Vice President of Professional Activities Yuki Tam, at vppa@dsp-nurho.com for more information.
Delta Sigma Pi

Delta Sigma Pi is a professional fraternity organized to foster the study of business in universities. To promote scholarship, social activity and the association of students for their mutual advancement by research and practice.
